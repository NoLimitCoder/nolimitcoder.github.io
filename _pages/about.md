I am a **NeurIPS Chair** for LXAI (2025). I have published 2 papers 1 @ **NeurIPS** (Lock-LLM) and 1 @ **ICML** (TAIG). I have also represented Dominican Republic at the **International Olympiad in Informatics** (**IOI 2020**). My research interests are in Machine/Deep learning, Optimization, Information Theory, AI Safety and Natural Language Processing (NLP).

I am also a Software Engineer at **Amazon Web Services** (**AWS**), and have just received my BCS Honours in Computer Science, minoring in Economics and Quantitative Finance.
